About DR Paints & Chemicals

At DR Paints and Chemicals, we specialize in manufacturing and wholesaling high-quality paints and industrial chemicals, offering innovative, durable, and sustainable solutions for homes, businesses, and industrial applications. Our premium paints and coatings are formulated for vibrant, long-lasting finishes, while our industrial-grade chemicals, including Epoxy, Turpentine, and Ethanol, meet the highest standards of reliability and performance. Committed to eco-friendly practices, we provide low VOC paints and safe chemical formulations, ensuring sustainability without compromising on quality. With a wide range of colors, textures, and chemical compositions tailored to specific needs, we take pride in our legacy of excellence and customer satisfaction. Trusted since 2017, DR Paints and Chemicals continues to bring innovation and color together, transforming every project with unmatched quality and expertise.
